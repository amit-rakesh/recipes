Ingredients:
 -2 tablespoons olive oil
 -2 cloves garlic, minced
 -1 (28 ounce) can whole peeled tomatoes
 -1/2 cup chopped fresh basil
 -salt and pepper to taste
 -1 pound penne pasta
 -1 pint heavy cream

 
Directions:
 1.In a large skillet over medium heat, cook garlic in olive oil until tender, 1 to 2 minutes. Stir in tomatoes, 
   breaking up a bit with fork. Stir in basil, salt and pepper and simmer 15 minutes. Stir in vodka and cook 15 
   minutes more.

 2.Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.

 3.Stir cream into sauce and cook 10 minutes more. Toss with hot pasta.
